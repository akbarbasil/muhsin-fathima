❓ Frequently Asked

Quick answers to your questions!

When is the RSVP deadline? +

Please RSVP by December 15th for smooth arrangements and catering.

What should I wear? +

Elegant cultural dress is encouraged. Light, modest clothing is recommended for the warm coastal weather. Traditional Islamic or South Indian attire would be perfect!

What's the weather like in December? +

Pleasant coastal weather! Daytime: 28-30°C, Evening: 22-25°C with refreshing sea breeze. December is perfect for visiting!

Will food be provided? +

Yes! Lunch will be served after the Nikkah ceremony featuring traditional Kayalpattinam cuisine and local specialties.

Can I take photos? +

Yes, but please respect everyone's privacy during this religious occasion. Professional photographers will cover main events.

Where should I park? +

Free parking available near both venues. Our coordination team will guide you to designated areas.
